Our Story

Four people, one taphouse (now two).

The name is a nod to J.R.R. Tolkien's old Ent, Treebeard. Patient, rooted, not in a hurry. That felt right for the kind of place we wanted to build.

We're Dave, Betsy, Luke, and Hanna. We came to Oregon from Wisconsin, fell hard for the forests and the beer scene, and opened the first Treebeerd's in Corvallis in August 2019. We spread our roots to Portland in April 2022.

This is a family-owned beer bar, not a chain or a concept. Come in, sit in the green velvet sofa, order a flight, and stay a while.

Glass growlers and outside food are always welcome at both spots. That's not a policy, it's just who we are.
